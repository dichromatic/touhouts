Instructions:

Welcome to a Bullet Hell Shmup inspired Space Invaders

Control your character with the arrow keys, and fire with F.

To spawn enemies, press either Q or W to choose your difficulty.

Your character's position wraps around the screen so be careful!

If you get hit by a bullet or an alien, the game is over.

Your hitbox is visible in the middle of the sprite.

Your player has 3 shields each with 3 HP.

HP is lost by the shields touching a bullet or an alien.

Each HP lost will decrease the size of the shield.

Aliens will disappear as soon as your bullet hits them.

You win the game once all of the aliens on the screen have been wiped.

You can reset the game by doing ctrl-R
