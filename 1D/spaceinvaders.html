Instructions:

Welcome to Space Invaders!

Control your character with the left and right arrow keys, and fire with F.

Your character's position wraps around the screen so be careful!

If you get hit by a bullet or an alien, the game is over.

Your hitbox is visible in the middle of the sprite.

Your shields will last for 5 hits.

Aliens will disappear as soon as your bullet / shield hits them.

You win the game once all of the aliens on the screen have been wiped.

You can reset the game by pressing r.
